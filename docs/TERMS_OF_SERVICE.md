## 2. Description of Service

### 2.1 What Bilu Store Is

Bilu Store is a **local classified marketplace** that lets individuals and businesses:
- Post ad listings for items and services they want to sell
- Browse and search listings posted by others
- Contact sellers and buyers through the in-app real-time chat
- Rate and review sellers they have transacted with
- Save (favorite) listings for later reference
- Boost listings with premium placement features

Bilu Store is built for users primarily in **Ethiopia**, supporting listings in Ethiopian Birr (ETB) and US Dollars (USD).
